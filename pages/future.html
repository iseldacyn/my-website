What's to come?

Additionally, I wish to learn some new programming languages and create projects with them. Languages I want to use are Zig, Go, Rust, Typescript, and Ruby.

I love teaching and math, and really want to study more math and teach it to those who may not understand it well. I wish to be a teacher students look up to, and want to help them understand something they may not be comfortable with otherwise! In the wise words of Chef Gusteau from Ratatouille, "Anyone can [learn mathematics]."

I have many passions and a lot I want to accomplish but feel I won't have enough time in my life to get it all done. I will certainly try my best to finish these all, though, and will try my best to have fun in the mean time!
